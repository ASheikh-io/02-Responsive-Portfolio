About Me


A current masters candidate for Policy, Planning and Management at the USM Muskie School of Public Service interested in furthering and expanding my skillset in a tech driven world. I have always been interested in the world of tech but have never had the opportunity to exercise my interests formally. Coming from a background in social services and policy work, I aim to become a full stack software developer and create bridges between social services and the world of tech. With UNH's Coding Bootcamp, I envision this dream becoming more and more a reality.


I am an aspiring Full Stack Web Developer. I have a passion for creating things be it through ink, clay, or code. My ultimate goal is to start my own Cyber Security firm after completing my Bachelors of Science in Computer Science.

I currently work full time for Comcast as a CAE 4 handling high security accounts for our customers that require additional identity security.

In my personal life I am a new mother to a baby boy. I spend the majority of my time working on personal coding projects with my son strapped to my chest.
